FAQs

Most asked questions:

Is there an Android App/Windows Program?

Unfortunately not, since we use the Apple iCloud to store the links.

Do we at LinkHub have access to your data?

No, your data is stored securely on your iCloud.

Will LinkHub be free forever?

LinkHub is being developed by university students. Unfortunately we don't have the resources to offer LinkHub for free indefinetly. We will not, however, make it more than the lowest amount that we can ask for on the App Store. We made LinkHub to be accessible for everyone, and we want to keep it that way.

How can I support LinkHub?

Tell your friends!

How can I contact you concerning bugs/features?

Go to our contacts page and shoot us an email, or do it within the app!
